Frequently Asked Questions

What is NexusGuard Protocol?

NexusGuard is a decentralized insurance protocol built on Sonic blockchain that provides comprehensive coverage against smart contract vulnerabilities, technical failures, and other DeFi-specific risks.

How does the risk assessment work?

NexusGuard uses a sophisticated multi-factor analysis model that considers audit scores, TVL, protocol complexity, market conditions, and historical performance to calculate risk scores dynamically.
